Description:
Santiago was born in Guatemala and moved to the US for a better life.

Transcript:

This is the story of my family. I have four people in my family, my dad, my mom, my sister, and I. Our relationship is very close. We always have each other with everything. We have always been a united family, and we will always be there to support each other. My mom and my dad met at the school of champerico, a rural community with many inhabitants near the sea. We live with my mother, after my father immigrated to the United States to give us a better life . We had many good and bad experiences,but the worst was living behind our family in Guatemala. They play an important role in our lives . Also, my older sister stayed in Guatemala because she did not want to come to the United States. We emigrated to the United States because the economy and social situation in my country is getting worse every day. That's why we made the decision with my mother to come to the United States. We went through all of Mexico by bus and car day and night for a week and It was very fast until we reached the border of the United States and Mexico to be able to be processed by immigration. My family changed a lot over time because people are while other pass away. We have always stayed together despite the circumstances. My maternal family has always there for us and although everything changes we are always communicate. I want my family to know that i will always be for them, an they will never be alone. Thanks to them i am the person who is fulfilling a dream of being able to be in the United States, and be able to continue over come new challenges. Remember that your family will always be for you; even if you stumble, they will always help you get up. You keep going and do not stop. I'm grateful to my dad and mom for giving me life, and i will always make them feel proud of their son.
