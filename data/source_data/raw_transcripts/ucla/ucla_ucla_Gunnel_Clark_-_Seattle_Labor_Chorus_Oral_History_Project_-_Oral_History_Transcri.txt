I went the mathematical line. Math was my very, very favorite subject. I remember, since I very seldom went

really sad. 


When I finished gymnasium, then you choose your path in life. My father said to me, ““You’d better become a 
teacher because you’re not smart enough to go to university.” So that’s what I did. [chuckles] Like my mother. 
She was a teacher, too, an elementary school teacher. So, kind of a sad state of affairs.
