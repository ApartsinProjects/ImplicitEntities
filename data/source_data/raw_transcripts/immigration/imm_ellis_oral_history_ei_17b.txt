Source: Ellis Island Oral History Collection (NPS)
Title: Catherine Remembers Mother Learning English
URL: https://www.nps.gov/elis/learn/education/oral-history-ei-17b.htm
Type: Immigrant Oral History
============================================================

Catherine Gaetano Gallippi recalls her mother’s efforts and struggles in learning English.

Gallippi (Her Mother Learns English): Sigrist: Did your mother ever learn English? GALLI Gallippi: Not fully. She tried very hard. She could write her name, she could tell time, money, everything. She knew everything about that, but she never spoke fluently...I do remember that they were trying to get a lot of these women at that time to take classes but they said, "We're too busy with the children. We just can't take time." So this is why a lot of people did not learn.
