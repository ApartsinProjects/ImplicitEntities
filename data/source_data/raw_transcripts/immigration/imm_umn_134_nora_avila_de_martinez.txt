Description:
Nora Avila de Martinez was born in Mexico. She left Mexico in 1998, and she and her husband moved to St. Paul that December. Since then, she has worked at the YMCA. She is also a student at Century College studying nursing.

Transcript:

Nora Avila de Martinez Transcription Hello. My name is Nora Martinez. I am from Mexico. On December 24, 1998 my husband and I we took the decision to move to Minnesota, looking for a better future. On Christmas Eve, we came to St. Paul, Minnesota and started our life together, far away from our families. It was [a] tough decision, but we needed to keep moving forward for our children, looking for better opportunities, better life. We do miss a lot of our family members but we need to keep moving. These days, when I decided to move to Minnesota, I brought with me something very special, which is my wedding dress. My wedding dress is very special because it was designed by my mother and her best friend. They worked a lot on it. They made it special for me. It might not cost a lot for others, for me it means a lot and it’s worth a lot because every single thing that was sewed on it, it was sewed by my mother’s hand. So this dress is very special for me. And I hope that one day, one of my daughters can wear it on their wedding day. Thank you so much for listening to my story.
