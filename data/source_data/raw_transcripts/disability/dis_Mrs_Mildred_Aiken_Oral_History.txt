Well,  I  believe  the  Elks  Club  still  has  the  Christmas  program  for 
the  children.  I  haven't  been  for  several  years  but  I  used  to  take

and  the  other.  And  Santa  Claus  would  give  out  candy  and  a  sock  full

place  for  a  long  time  at  Christmas  time.  It's  a  shame  it  is  a  nice 
thing  I  think.
